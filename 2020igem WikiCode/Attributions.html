Overview

The iGEM 2020 NWU-CHINA-A team is a family composed of many members, such as art group, experiment group, modeling group and webpage group. On this page, you can learn about everyone who contributed to our project. We would like to thank all the team advisors and teachers who helped us in this project. Besides, we would like to thank teams from other schools who helped us and gave us very useful suggestions. Finally, we would like to thank all the companies, schools and people from all walks of life who have supported us.

Exchange meeting

In The 4th Southern China Regional Online Meeting, we learned about changes in competition rules and award criteria because of the epidemic, and sought experience from seniors who participated in the competition in previous years. In the Eastern China iGEM Meetup, we conducted the first project show and received suggestions from various teams. In Shaanxi iGEM Meetup, we set the future cooperation direction with other teams. In Friend Team Meetup, we communicated the experimental process with many teams that also do AMP related projects. In the 7th Conference of China iGEMer Meetup, we had the honor to communicate with more teams.
